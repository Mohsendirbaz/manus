# EPU Strategy Website — Design Brainstorm

## Context
A searchable, premium website presenting the EPU Strategy concept: physics-certified autonomous safety compute (EPU chip), hydrogen driverless platform, sensor suite constitution, and TEA/LCA governance. Audience: engineers, executives, regulators, and strategic partners.

<idea>
**Design Movement:** Technical Brutalism meets Engineering Precision

**Core Principles:**
1. Monochromatic graphite-and-white base with single vivid accent (electric cyan) — restraint as sophistication
2. Grid-based layout with deliberate asymmetry — engineering blueprints translated into UI
3. Typography as structure — large weight-contrast headings anchor every section
4. Data-forward hierarchy — numbers, labels, and callouts treated as primary visual elements

**Color Philosophy:** Deep graphite (#1C1F26) background signals authority and seriousness. Pure white text for primary content. Electric cyan (#00D4C8) for interactive elements and data highlights. Amber (#F59E0B) for warnings, caveats, and risk indicators — mirrors the TEA/LCA governance color language from the slides.

**Layout Paradigm:** Left-anchored sidebar navigation with fixed section labels. Main content flows in a two-column grid: image/diagram on left, text/data on right. Hero section uses full-bleed dark background with a large typographic statement.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rule dividers with section numbering (01, 02, 03…) — engineering document aesthetic
- Monospace font for technical labels, callouts, and code-like data (WCET, kgCO2e, etc.)
- Subtle grid overlay on hero background — blueprint paper texture

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal additional technical detail. Search highlights matching text with cyan underline. Smooth scroll between sections with section indicator in sidebar.

**Animation:** Entrance animations: text slides in from left, images fade in from right. No bounce, no spring — linear or ease-out only. Section transitions: subtle opacity fade.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Space Grotesk Bold 700 — geometric, technical, authoritative
- Body: Inter Regular 400 — readable, neutral
- Monospace labels: JetBrains Mono — for technical callouts

## Selected Approach: **Premium OEM Concept Study — Automotive Strategy Whitepaper**

This approach best matches the source material's tone: boardroom-ready, engineering-review-grade, and authority-signaling. The warm off-white background and serif/sans-serif pairing evoke premium strategy documents and OEM concept studies. The muted teal accent aligns with the cyan/teal color language from the generated images. The editorial layout gives each of the 30 slides room to breathe while maintaining a searchable, navigable structure.
